We read the product warning emails sent by Amazon to SellerRunning users and add them to the common brand pool. In this way, we aim to minimize "Policy Warning" notifications that may come from Amazon in the future and to make your account safer by detecting problematic brands/products in advance, thanks to other SellerRunning users even if they are not in your intentory. You can add brands to your Brand black list by selecting the brands from the pool.

error_outlineImportant: In order for these features to work propoerly, please make sure that you have completed the steps in the Feedback Settings for all your stores completely.
